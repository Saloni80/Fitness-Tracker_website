Water is essential for maintaining good health and overall well-being. It plays a vital role in nearly every bodily function, including regulating body temperature, aiding digestion, transporting nutrients, flushing out toxins, and keeping joints lubricated. Staying well-hydrated helps maintain energy levels, supports brain function, and promotes healthy skin. Water also assists in weight management by promoting satiety and improving metabolism. Dehydration, even in mild forms, can lead to fatigue, headaches, and reduced physical and mental performance.
